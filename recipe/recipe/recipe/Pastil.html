How to Make Chicken Pastil: A Step-by-Step Guide

Chicken Pastil is a traditional Filipino dish featuring seasoned shredded chicken wrapped in banana leaves and steamed to perfection. It's flavorful, aromatic, and often served with rice for a complete meal. In this blog post, I’ll guide you through making Chicken Pastil step by step.

Ingredients:

  • For the Chicken Filling:
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken (you can use boiled or roasted chicken)
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 thumb-sized piece of ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • For Wrapping and Steaming:
  • Banana leaves (cut into squares, about 6x6 inches)
  • Toothpicks or kitchen twine (for securing the wraps)
  • Steamer or large pot with a steaming rack

Step 1: Prepare the Chicken Filling

  • Cook and Shred Chicken: If you haven’t already, cook and shred the chicken. Season it lightly with salt and pepper.
  • Sauté Aromatics: In a skillet or frying pan, heat vegetable oil over medium heat. Sauté the chopped onion until translucent, then add the minced garlic and grated ginger. Cook until fragrant, about 1-2 minutes.
  • Add Chicken and Seasonings: Add the shredded chicken to the skillet. Stir in soy sauce, oyster sauce (if using), brown sugar, and ground black pepper. Mix well and cook for another 3-5 minutes, stirring occasionally. Adjust seasoning with salt if needed. Remove from heat and let the filling cool slightly.

Step 2: Prepare Banana Leaves

  • Blanch Banana Leaves: If using fresh banana leaves, quickly blanch them over an open flame or dip them in boiling water to soften and make them pliable. This step also helps to eliminate any bitterness.
  • Cut and Prepare Wraps: Cut the banana leaves into squares of about 6x6 inches. Wipe them clean and lightly brush with oil to prevent sticking.

Step 3: Wrap the Chicken Pastil

  • Assemble the Wraps: Place a spoonful of the chicken filling in the center of each banana leaf square.
  • Fold and Secure: Fold the banana leaf over the filling to form a rectangle or square packet. Secure the edges with toothpicks or tie with kitchen twine to keep the packet closed.

Step 4: Steam the Chicken Pastil

  • Prepare Steamer: Arrange the wrapped Chicken Pastil packets in a steamer basket or steaming rack.
  • Steam: Steam the packets over medium heat for about 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken filling is heated through and the banana leaves have imparted their aroma to the dish.

Step 5: Serve

  • Unwrap and Serve: Carefully unwrap the Chicken Pastil packets and transfer them to serving plates.
  • Enjoy: Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy this delicious and aromatic Filipino dish!

Making Chicken Pastil is a wonderful way to experience traditional Filipino flavors. Follow these steps, and you’ll create a delightful meal that’s perfect for sharing with family and friends. Enjoy your Chicken Pastil!
