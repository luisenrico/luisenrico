How to Make Chicken Curry: A Step-by-Step Guide

Chicken curry is a flavorful dish enjoyed in various cuisines around the world, featuring tender chicken simmered in a rich and aromatic curry sauce. In this blog post, I’ll guide you through making delicious chicken curry step by step.

Ingredients:

  • For the Chicken Marinade:
  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • For the Curry Sauce:
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated or minced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste (optional, for extra richness)
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 cup coconut milk or heavy cream (for a richer sauce)
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Step 1: Marinate the Chicken

  • Prepare the Marinade: In a mixing bowl, combine the yogurt, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  • Marinate the Chicken: Add the chicken pieces to the marinade, making sure each piece is well coated. Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld.

Step 2: Prepare the Curry Sauce

  • Heat Oil: In a large skillet or pot, heat vegetable oil or ghee over medium heat.
  • Sauté Aromatics: Add the chopped onion and sauté until softened and translucent, about 5-7 minutes. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger, and sauté for another 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  • Add Spices: Stir in the ground cumin, ground coriander, turmeric powder, and chili powder. Cook the spices for about 1 minute until they become fragrant, stirring constantly to prevent burning.
  • Add Tomatoes: Add the chopped tomatoes and tomato paste (if using). Cook for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes break down and the mixture thickens slightly.

Step 3: Cook the Chicken

  • Add Chicken: Add the marinated chicken pieces to the skillet, along with any remaining marinade. Stir well to coat the chicken in the spice mixture.
  • Simmer: Cook the chicken for 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it is cooked through and tender.

Step 4: Finish the Curry

  • Add Coconut Milk or Cream: Pour in the coconut milk or heavy cream, stirring gently to combine. Bring the curry to a simmer and cook for another 5-7 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken slightly.
  • Adjust Seasoning: Taste the curry and adjust the salt and chili powder as needed to suit your preference.

Step 5: Serve

  • Garnish: Remove the curry from heat. Garnish with freshly chopped cilantro for added freshness and flavor.
  • Serve: Serve the chicken curry hot, accompanied by steamed rice, naan bread, or your favorite side dishes.

Making chicken curry at home allows you to customize the flavors and enjoy a comforting and aromatic meal. Follow these steps, and you’ll have a delicious chicken curry that’s perfect for any occasion. Enjoy!
