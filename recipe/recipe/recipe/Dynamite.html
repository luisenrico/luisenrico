How to Make Dynamite Lumpia: A Step-by-Step Guide

Dynamite Lumpia are spicy and delicious snacks that are perfect for parties or gatherings. Here’s how you can make them at home:

Ingredients:

  • 10-12 large green chili peppers (such as Anaheim or jalapeño peppers)
  • 100 grams ground pork or ground chicken
  • 50 grams cheese (cheddar or mozzarella), cut into thin strips
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Spring roll wrappers (lumpia wrappers)
  • Vegetable oil, for frying
  • Toothpicks or skewers, for securing

Step 1: Prepare the Chili Peppers

  • Prepare Peppers: Wash the chili peppers thoroughly. Make a lengthwise slit on one side of each pepper, starting from the stem down to the tip. Remove the seeds and membranes using a small spoon or knife, taking care to keep the peppers intact.

Step 2: Prepare the Filling

  • Cook Filling: In a skillet or frying pan, heat a small amount of oil over medium heat. Sauté chopped onions and minced garlic until translucent and fragrant.
  • Add Ground Meat: Add ground pork or chicken to the skillet. Cook until the meat is fully browned and cooked through, breaking it up into smaller pieces with a spatula.
  • Season: Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat and let the filling cool slightly.

Step 3: Stuff the Peppers

  • Fill Peppers: Using a small spoon or your fingers, stuff each chili pepper with the cooked meat mixture. Insert a strip of cheese into the center of each pepper.

Step 4: Wrap with Spring Roll Wrappers

  • Wrap Peppers: Carefully wrap each stuffed chili pepper with a spring roll wrapper, making sure to seal the edges with a bit of water to prevent the filling from leaking out.
  • Secure with Toothpicks: Secure the ends of the spring roll wrappers with toothpicks or skewers to keep them closed and secure.

Step 5: Fry the Dynamite Lumpia

  • Heat Oil: In a deep frying pan or pot, heat enough vegetable oil over medium-high heat for deep-frying (around 2 inches deep).
  • Fry Dynamite Lumpia: Carefully place the wrapped chili peppers in the hot oil, a few at a time, depending on the size of your frying pan. Fry until the spring roll wrappers are golden brown and crispy, turning occasionally to ensure even cooking.
  • Drain: Remove the fried Dynamite Lumpia from the oil using a slotted spoon or tongs. Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil.

Step 6: Serve

  • Serve Hot: Serve Dynamite Lumpia hot as an appetizer or snack. Remove the toothpicks or skewers before serving.
  • Dip: Serve with your favorite dipping sauce, such as sweet chili sauce or vinegar with garlic and onions.

Enjoy your homemade Dynamite Lumpia, filled with spicy peppers and savory filling wrapped in crispy spring roll wrappers!
